city life and resort living converge. Sprawling across 105

world-class amenities. Built on the philosophy of 
sustainable design and this integrated township features 
landscaped parks and thoughtfully planned residential 
clusters that redeﬁne community living. From wide 
internal roads to modern recreational infrastructure, 
every detail reﬂects comfort and consciousness.
