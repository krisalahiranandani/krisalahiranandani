EVERLYN

THE SOUL OF TRANQUILITY

A Krisala × Hiranandani Masterpiece

An Oasis Built for Everyday Peace

Everlyn is the soul of the township, a tranquil retreat crafted for those who seek balance, beauty, and inner calm. Surrounded by lotus ponds, butterfly gardens, and wellness zones, it’s a space where nature and nurture coexist.

Whether it’s a morning meditation or an evening under the stars, Everlyn invites you to live gently, every day. From Zen gardens to soundscape healing zones, every corner of Everlyn is designed to soothe the senses and quiet the mind.

With yoga decks, spa retreats, and digital detox zones, Everlyn promotes holistic health—physical, emotional, and spiritual.

  • Nature-Infused Living: Forest trails & Biofiltration wetlands.
  • Wellness First: Dedicated Yoga decks & Zen pods.
  • Grand Infrastructure: Vehicular-free podium & Pedestrian bridge.
